Coconut Rice Bowls

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Coconut milk
  • Aromatics, Herbs, and Spices: ginger, garlic, lemongrass, cilantro, mint
  • Protein: tofu (or chicken or shrimp)
  • Vegetables: asparagus, green onions, peas
  • Coconut oil
  • Soy Sauce

Cooking instructions

  1. Cook the rice: Add rice, coconut milk, water, curry powder, salt, and 1 teaspoon of ginger. Bring to a simmer. And cook for 20 minutes.
  2. Make the Sauce: Whisk together sriracha, soy sauce, and coconut sugar.
  3. Pat tofu dry and cut into thick strips.
  4. Sear Tofu (or cook chicken, fish or shrimp if using): Heat a pan to med-high (we like cast iron).
  5. Sauté Veggies: In the same pan that you cooked the tofu in add 1 tablespoon coconut oil sauté asparagus (or other vegetables) for about 1 minute.
  6. Combine: Add tofu, sauce, peas, and lime juice, stirring to combine. Take off the heat.
